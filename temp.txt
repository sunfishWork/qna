Here are five of the most well-known specification documents related to 5G:

1. **3GPP TS 38.101 - "NR; Physical layer; General description"**
   - This document defines the physical layer (PHY) specifications for the 5G New Radio (NR) air interface. It is one of the foundational documents for 5G wireless communication.

2. **3GPP TS 23.501 - "System Architecture for the 5G System"**
   - This document describes the overall architecture of the 5G system, including the interactions between the user equipment (UE), access network, and core network.

3. **ITU-R IMT-2020 - "Requirements for Next Generation Mobile Networks"**
   - This report by the International Telecommunication Union (ITU) defines the requirements and performance metrics for 5G networks, including aspects like data rates, latency, and network density.

4. **ETSI EN 302 576-1 - "Radio transmission and reception; Mobile radio telecommunications systems; Base stations operating in frequency bands from 87 MHz to 915 MHz"**
   - This document provides specifications for base station equipment used in 5G networks, including requirements for radio transmission and reception.

5. **IEEE 802.11ax - "Amendment to IEEE Std 802.11-2016: High Efficiency WLAN"**
   - While primarily focused on Wi-Fi, this document is closely related to 5G as it defines high-efficiency wireless local area network (WLAN) standards that complement 5G fixed wireless access.
